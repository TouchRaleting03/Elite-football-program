Welcome to Elite football program

This is an elite program which specializes in working with a small number of boys per age group. The program focuses on 3 components to develop a player and a man for the future. Fitness, nutrition, and football skill. The program's main focus is long term athlete development. We have 3 major events in a year. 1 National tournament, 1x International tournament and an academy tour.
