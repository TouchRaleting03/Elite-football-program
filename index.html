Welcome to Elite football program

This is an elite program which caters for only 22 boys in the age 12-13 years old. The program focuses on 3 components to hence and develop a player and man for the future. Fitness, nutrition and football skill. The program runs for a year and has 3 major events in the year. 2 national tournaments and 1 intensive training camp and a visit with elite academies
